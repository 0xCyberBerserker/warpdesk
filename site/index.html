Why it exists

Linux WARP works. The desktop experience needed discipline.

WarpDesk does not try to replace the official backend. It wraps warp-cli and warp-svc in a tighter, more readable GUI for people who prefer operational clarity over command memorization.

What ships today
  • Connection state, protocol and mode control
  • Profile save, apply, and delete workflow
  • Diagnostics view and tray shortcuts
  • English, Spanish, and Catalan support
  • Palette-aware Qt styling for Linux desktops
